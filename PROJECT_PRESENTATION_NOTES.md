# Project Presentation Notes

## Project title
Coffee Shop Sales & Customer Behavior Intelligence Dashboard

## Objective
To help the client understand revenue performance, product contribution, customer demand patterns and operational improvement opportunities using transaction-level coffee shop sales data.

## Tools used
- Python
- Pandas
- Plotly
- Streamlit
- Power BI for business reporting reference

## Key business questions answered
- What are the top revenue-driving products?
- Which product categories contribute most to revenue?
- Which store locations perform strongest?
- What are the peak demand hours?
- Which time slots require stronger staffing?
- Which products/categories should be promoted or reviewed?
- How can inventory and staffing be optimized?

## Key features
- Dynamic date, store, category, product type and time-slot filters
- Executive KPI cards
- Revenue trend analysis
- Product intelligence page
- Customer demand heatmap
- Store workload matrix
- Business recommendation page
- Data QA and export center

## Suggested explanation
This dashboard is designed as a client-ready analytics solution, not only a static report. It updates automatically when new data is uploaded and provides business insights across sales, products, customer behavior and operations.
